# Get It Done — Practical PM Guide for Irish Small Businesses & Charities

> A free, interactive project management guide built on the **ECCSR framework** by Joe Houghton, UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School.

🌐 **Live site:** [getitdonepm.github.io](https://getitdonepm.github.io)

---

## What this is

A single-page web application covering every area of project management relevant to Irish small businesses and charities. Built as part of an MSc Project Management individual assignment at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School.

The guide is practical, not academic. Every chapter uses real Irish business and charity examples, connects to a named academic framework, and includes tools you can use immediately.

---

## The ECCSR Framework

All 14 chapters are viewed through the **ECCSR lens** developed by Joe Houghton (2023):

| Letter | Principle | What it means in practice |
|--------|-----------|--------------------------|
| **E** | Ethical | Ethics is the lens for every decision, not a section you tick at the start |
| **C** | Collaborative | Genuine co-creation — Edmondson (1999): psychological safety built deliberately |
| **C** | Creating | Projects require creativity and judgement, not just process compliance |
| **S** | Sustainable | Design for the after — what remains when the funding ends? |
| **R** | Results | Measurable outcomes for real people, not just activity reports |

## Features

- **14 full chapters** with rich visual content — cards, timelines, accordions, stats, comparison tables
- **8 ECCSR-aligned AI prompts** built for Irish organisations with copy-paste functionality
- **Irish business examples** throughout — Galway, Cork, Limerick, Waterford, Meath, Donegal
- **GDPR guidance** specific to the Data Protection Commission Ireland
- **Knowledge quiz** — 10 questions across all 14 chapters
- **NotebookLM podcast scripts** — 14 two-host conversation scripts ready to generate audio
- **Video scripts** — 14 screen-recording scripts with voiceover and visual direction
- **Free downloadable templates** — Gantt, RACI, Risk Register, Project Charter, Stakeholder Map
- **AI assistant chatbot** — powered by Claude, ECCSR-framed, Irish context
- **PM tools comparison** — ClickUp, Notion, Trello, Asana, Monday, with Irish pricing
- **Custom cursor, particle animations, smooth scroll** — built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS

---

## Technology

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step

- Google Fonts — Fraunces (display), Plus Jakarta Sans (body), JetBrains Mono (labels)
- AI chatbot powered by Anthropic Claude API
