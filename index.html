The Framework

The ECCSR Model

Built on the ECCSR framework by Joe Houghton from UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School, this guide follows five key dimensions: Envision, Create, Communicate, Sustain, and Review.

Framework Overview

Envision: Define your vision and objectives. Create: Plan and organize resources. Communicate: Keep stakeholders informed. Sustain: Maintain momentum and manage risks. Review: Learn and improve.
